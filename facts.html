Facts & Observations

After visting many round-abouts in Kanpur itself, we collected the facts that :-

  1. For a 2 lane road people tend to form mostly 3 psuedo-lanes.
  2. The leftmost lane is always in motion.
  3. While the other two lanes are under the control of Traffic Siganl.
  4. The Trucks, Tankers and Trollers mostly are found during night hours (10pm to 6am)
  5. Also, during 11pm to 4am Orange light glows, which symbolises First come First Serve Traffic Movement
  6. Further we observed that Trollers occupied the maximum road space, followed by Tankers, Trucks & Buses, Cars, E-rickshaws & Tempos and lastly Bikes, Scooties and Cycles.
  7. Correspondingly, Bikes & Scooties move at the fastest pickup speed followed by Cars, Tempos, E-rickshaw, Bus, unloaded Tanker then comes unloaded Troller, Truck and lastly comes loaded Troller, Loaded Tanker and Cycle.
  8. So with this and along with some approximations we concluded that the size of each vehicle can be measured in units where each unit is equal to 2.5 meters
    • Trucks & Buses = 5u
    • Loaded & Unloaded Tankers = 6u
    • Loaded & Unloaded Trollers = 7u
    • Cars, Tempos & E-rickshaw = 2u
    • Bikes, Scooties & Cycles = 1u
  10. Similarly the road length is also expressed in units(u) and space consumed.
